Home Castle Painting Inc.

Bonded and Insured, License #935506

Local Painting Company Based in the Greater Los Angeles Area

Just as people look for paint to bring a sense of newness and freshness to their homes, we at Home Castle Painting believe that we offer a fresh approach to the business of painting.

Home Castle Painting was established in 2008 by Eddy Cabrera. Eddy has over 20 years of experience doing interior/exterior, previously painting for other contractors. In the years since 2008, a reputation based on fine work and responsibility has been cemented by our team's work ethic. Our work involves exterior and interior painting of all homes of all sizes and styles.

Detail and craftsmanship are important to Home Castle Painting. We take the time to thoroughly prepare and powersand each surface to be painted according to it's needs. All materials and products are carefully selected and of the highest quality.
